Peplocker

Privacy Policy

Last updated on 12th December, 2020

  • You login to Peplocker via your Google account and Peplocker uses your own Google Drive to keep your notes data.
  • Peplocker can not read any data in your drive account, other than files and folders that it creates itself.
  • No data is ever stored locally on your device. Neither your notes nor your google account data
  • You only sign in with your password which is used as the key for a symmetric AES 256 bit encryption - an industry standard.
  • When you update your notes, the data is encrypted in realtime and saved in your Google Drive account without being saved locally. This introduces a bit of time delay while saving the changes, but maintains security.
  • To lock the app, simply exit from it and when you try to open it again, it will prompt for the password.
  • When you lock your phone screen, Peplocker automatically locks you out, thereby adding more security.

This privacy policy can be updated in future.
